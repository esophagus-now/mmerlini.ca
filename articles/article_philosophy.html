Philosophy on Writing Technical Articles

For better or for worse, I do everything my own way.

A lot of my life revolves around technical articles, which I define as "documents that are trying to explain something technical". This includes (but is not limited to):

  • Anything trying to explain computer concepts
  • Instruction manuals
  • API documentation
  • Programming tutorials
  • Anything I have put into the Articles section of this site

Many technical articles exhibit one or more Unforgivable Impediments to Understanding, which are detailed below. When I write my own articles, I do my best to eliminate these impediments. However, nobody's perfect; if you ever find anything that you don't understand, just get in contact with me and I'll try to fix it.

Following the Unforgivable Impediments, there is a short list of Forgivable Impediments. Finally, I end this article with a list of Good Things that your articles easier to read.
